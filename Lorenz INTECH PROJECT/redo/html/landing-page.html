College of Education

The College of Education at Central Luzon State University (CLSU) is a prestigious institution known for its commitment to providing top-notch teacher education. This center of excellence is dedicated to preparing the next generation of educators who can deliver high-quality instruction to students, thus contributing to the development of the education sector in the Philippines. The College of Education at CLSU is dedicated to maintaining a high standard of academic excellence. Faculty members are not only experienced educators but also active researchers who contribute to the field of education. They ensure that students are exposed to the latest teaching methodologies and research findings. Furthermore, the college emphasizes hands-on experience through practicum and teaching internships, allowing students to apply what they have learned in real classroom settings. This practical exposure ensures that graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of the teaching profession.

Dr. Regidor G. Gaboy

Dr. Regidor G. Gaboy. The Dean of The College of Education is a graduate of Doctor of Philosophy in Development Education from Central Luzon State University. His research interests include the areas of Educational Leadership and Management, Development Education, Technology and Livelihood Education and Technology in Education.
